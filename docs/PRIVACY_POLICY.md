Last updated: 2026-04-16

## Privacy Policy

### 1. Who We Are

JumpChain Browser ("the App") is a desktop application for browsing JumpChain PDFs from community Google Drives.

Owner/Developer: `ByEriX`  
Contact: `madebyerix@gmail.com` or `inusagi.neko@unibyerix.moe`

### 2. Information We Collect

JumpChain Browser is a local desktop app. It processes the following data:

- Google OAuth credentials/tokens stored locally so you can stay signed in
- Local app data you create (bookmarks, bookmark notes, local download path metadata, app settings)
- Local thumbnail cache files and local downloaded PDF files on your device

### 3. How We Use Information

We use this data only to:

- Authenticate with Google using OAuth
- Read Google Drive content using read-only access needed for browse/sync/thumbnail/download features
- Store and show your bookmarks, notes, and settings locally
- Cache thumbnails and track local downloaded copies for convenience

### 4. Google API Services User Data Policy

JumpChain Browser's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs to any other app will adhere to the [Google API Services User Data Policy](https://developers.google.com/terms/api-services-user-data-policy), including the Limited Use requirements.

### 5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data.

This app does not use a developer-operated backend server for syncing your personal app data. Data is processed locally on your device and sent only to Google services when you use Google-dependent features.

We disclose information only when necessary to:

- Communicate with Google APIs you explicitly authorize
- Comply with applicable law, regulation, legal process, or enforceable government request

### 6. Data Storage and Retention

- App data is stored locally on your device.
- OAuth credentials/tokens are stored locally and retained until you sign out, clear app data, or remove the app data manually.
- Local downloads are stored in your Documents folder under `JumpChain Archive` unless removed by you.

### 7. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data. No method of storage or transmission is 100% secure, so absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

### 8. Your Choices and Controls

You can:

- Revoke the App's Google access in your Google Account permissions
- Stop using the App at any time
- Delete local App data from your device (including database, cache, and downloaded files)

### 9. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

### 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version with a new "Last updated" date.
